A Small Tribute to Subhash Chandra Bose

  • 1897-Born in Cuttack Orissa Division,Bengal Province
  • 1918-Passed BA from Scotish Church College under University of Calcutta
  • 1919-Left fro Europe to study for ICS Exam
  • 1921-Resigned from Civil Service and returned to India
  • 1923-Elected as president of All India Youth Congress
  • 1930-Elected as the Mayor of Calcutta
  • 1939-Re-elected as the President of the Indian National Congress
  • 1939-Formed All India Forward Block a Faction of Indian National Congress
  • 1941-Excape India for Germany via Afganisthan
  • 1943-Formed the Indian National Army
  • 1944-From a rally in Singapore addressed Gandhi as the Father of The Nation
  • 1944-Lost the battle against British in Imphal and Kohima

An army that has no tradition of courage,fearlessness and
invincibility cannot hold its own in a struggle with a powerful enemy
